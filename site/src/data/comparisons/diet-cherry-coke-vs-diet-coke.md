## Taste Comparison

Diet Cherry Coke and Diet Coke are closer in taste than any other comparison in the cherry soda space, and for good reason — **Diet Cherry Coke is literally Diet Coke with cherry flavoring added**. They share the exact same base formula, the same sweetener system, and the same carbonation profile. The only difference is the addition of natural cherry flavor in Diet Cherry Coke.

**Diet Coke** has a distinctive taste that sets it apart from other diet colas. Unlike Coke Zero Sugar, which aims to replicate the taste of regular Coca-Cola, Diet Coke has its own unique flavor identity. It is lighter, crisper, and has a slightly citrusy tang that has made it one of the best-selling diet sodas in the world since its launch in 1982. The clean, dry finish is a hallmark of the Diet Coke experience.

**Diet Cherry Coke** takes everything you know and love about Diet Coke and adds a layer of natural cherry flavoring. The cherry note is medium-intensity — present enough to be clearly noticeable but not so strong that it overwhelms the familiar Diet Coke base. It adds a fruity sweetness and a slightly rounder quality to the flavor profile. The finish is still clean and crisp, but with a pleasant cherry aftertaste that lingers briefly.

For Diet Coke loyalists, trying Diet Cherry Coke for the first time often feels like a revelation. It is unmistakably Diet Coke at its core, just with an added dimension of flavor. The cherry does not change the fundamental character of the drink — it enhances it. Think of it as Diet Coke wearing a cherry-scented accessory rather than a completely different outfit.

The carbonation and mouthfeel are virtually identical between the two products. Both have that signature Diet Coke effervescence — lively and crisp without being overly aggressive. Served over ice, both drinks deliver the same satisfying fizz and refreshment.

## Nutrition Comparison

The nutritional profiles of Diet Cherry Coke and Diet Coke are nearly identical, which makes sense given that they share the same base formula:

- **Calories**: Both have **0 calories** per 12 oz can
- **Sugar**: Both have **0g sugar**
- **Total Carbohydrates**: Both have **0g**
- **Sodium**: Both have approximately **40mg** per 12 oz can
- **Caffeine**: Both have **46mg** per 12 oz can
- **Sweetener**: Both use **aspartame**
- **Phosphoric Acid**: Present in both

The ingredient lists are virtually identical, with the sole exception being the natural flavors entry. Diet Cherry Coke includes cherry among its natural flavors, while Diet Coke does not. This addition does not change the calorie count, sugar content, or any other nutritional metric in a meaningful way.

Both products are free of fat, protein, and significant vitamins or minerals. They are designed to deliver the cola taste experience with zero caloric impact. For anyone tracking macros or counting calories, Diet Cherry Coke and Diet Coke are completely interchangeable from a nutritional standpoint.

## Price Comparison

Since Diet Cherry Coke and Diet Coke are both produced by Coca-Cola and distributed through the same channels, their pricing is essentially the same:

- **12-pack cans**: Both typically $5.48–$7.99
- **20 oz bottle**: Both typically $2.19–$2.99
- **2-liter bottle**: Both typically $1.98–$3.49

There is no consistent price premium for either product. Retailers price them identically in most cases, and they are often included in the same promotional deals. When your grocery store runs a "3 for $12" deal on Coca-Cola 12-packs, both Diet Coke and Diet Cherry Coke are usually eligible.

Bulk purchasing through warehouse clubs like Costco and Sam's Club offers the best per-can value for both products. Online retailers like Amazon also carry both at competitive prices, often with subscribe-and-save discounts available.

Since price is not a differentiator, your decision between these two products can be based entirely on taste preference without any financial consideration.

## Availability

This is the one area where Diet Coke has a clear and significant advantage. **Diet Coke is one of the most widely available beverages in the world.** You can find it at virtually every grocery store, convenience store, gas station, vending machine, restaurant, and fast-food chain in the United States and beyond. It is a staple of the American beverage landscape.

**Diet Cherry Coke** has more limited availability, particularly during its current relaunch phase. While it is already stocked at major retailers including Walmart, Kroger, Target, and Amazon, it is not yet as ubiquitous as Diet Coke. Smaller convenience stores, vending machines, and fountain drink stations are less likely to carry Diet Cherry Coke.

For those who want to ensure they can always find their preferred drink, Diet Coke is the safer bet. However, Diet Cherry Coke availability is expanding steadily, and stocking up when you find it is always an option. Check our [where to buy guide](/where-to-buy) for the latest information on Diet Cherry Coke retail locations.

Fountain availability is another consideration. Diet Coke is available at virtually every restaurant and fast-food chain with a Coca-Cola fountain system. Diet Cherry Coke fountain availability is much more limited, though some Coca-Cola Freestyle machines do offer it as an option.

## Which Should You Choose?

Since Diet Cherry Coke and Diet Coke are nutritionally identical and priced the same, the choice comes down to one simple question: do you want cherry flavor with your Diet Coke?

**Choose Diet Cherry Coke if you:**
- Love Diet Coke but want a little extra flavor variety
- Enjoy cherry-flavored beverages in general
- Want to add some excitement to your daily soda routine
- Like a slightly sweeter, fruitier taste profile
- Are looking for an alternative to plain Diet Coke without switching brands

**Choose Diet Coke if you:**
- Prefer the pure, unadorned Diet Coke taste
- Want maximum availability wherever you go
- Like the clean, crisp finish without any fruity notes
- Are a purist who appreciates the original formula
- Need reliable fountain drink access at restaurants

The best approach for many Diet Coke fans is to keep both on hand. Diet Coke serves as the reliable everyday choice — always available, always consistent. Diet Cherry Coke adds variety when you want something a little different without straying far from what you already love. Since there is no nutritional penalty for choosing one over the other, you can freely alternate based on your mood.

Many people who try Diet Cherry Coke find that it becomes their new default, while others enjoy it as an occasional treat alongside their regular Diet Coke. Either way, both products deliver the zero-calorie refreshment that Coca-Cola's diet lineup is known for.
