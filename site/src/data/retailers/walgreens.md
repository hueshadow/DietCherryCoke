## Availability Overview

Walgreens is a convenient option for picking up Diet Cherry Coke, particularly if you need a single-serve bottle or a 2-liter on short notice. As a pharmacy and convenience-focused retailer, Walgreens does not carry the same breadth of pack sizes as a full grocery store, but its widespread locations and extended hours make it a practical choice for quick purchases.

Availability at Walgreens is classified as limited because not every location stocks Diet Cherry Coke consistently. Stores in high-traffic urban areas and those with larger beverage sections are more likely to carry it, while smaller or more pharmacy-focused locations may only stock mainstream Coca-Cola products. If you are checking [whether Diet Cherry Coke is back](/is-it-back) in your area, Walgreens can be hit or miss, so verifying stock online or by phone before visiting is a good idea.

That said, Walgreens has over 8,700 locations across the United States, and many of them are open late or even 24 hours, making it a reliable fallback when grocery stores are closed.

Walgreens focuses primarily on single-serve and small-format beverages. Here is what you can expect to find and pay:

- **20 oz Bottle**: The most common format at Walgreens, typically priced between $2.29 and $2.79. These are usually available in the refrigerated cooler near the front of the store.
- **2-Liter Bottle**: Available at many locations for $2.49 to $3.49. Walgreens often runs promotions that bring the 2-liter price down to $1.99 or less.
- **12 oz Single Can**: Some locations carry individual cans for $1.29 to $1.79, though this is less common.
- **6-Pack of 16.9 oz Bottles**: Occasionally available for $4.99 to $5.99, though this format is not stocked at all locations.

Walgreens pricing on single-serve beverages is higher than what you would pay at a grocery store, which is typical for convenience-format retailers.
