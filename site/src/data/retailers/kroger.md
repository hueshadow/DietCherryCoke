## Digital Coupons & Loyalty Card Savings

Kroger's digital ecosystem is one of the best in the grocery industry for saving money on products like Diet Cherry Coke. Here is how to make the most of it:

**Kroger Plus Card**: This free loyalty card is the foundation of all Kroger savings. Simply scan it at checkout or enter your phone number to receive member pricing on thousands of items, including Coca-Cola products. Without the card, you will pay the non-member price, which can be noticeably higher.

**Digital Coupons**: The Kroger app and website feature a digital coupon section where you can clip coupons directly to your loyalty card. Coca-Cola frequently offers digital coupons through Kroger, such as $1 off a 12-pack or $0.50 off a 2-liter. These coupons stack with sale prices, so you can sometimes get exceptional deals.

**Fuel Points**: Every dollar you spend at Kroger earns fuel points, which translate to discounts at Kroger fuel stations. Buying Diet Cherry Coke contributes to your fuel point balance, and during promotional periods, Coca-Cola purchases may earn bonus fuel points (such as 4x points on select beverages).

**Personalized Offers**: Kroger's app uses your purchase history to generate personalized deals. If you buy Diet Cherry Coke regularly, you may start seeing targeted discounts and offers specifically for that product.
